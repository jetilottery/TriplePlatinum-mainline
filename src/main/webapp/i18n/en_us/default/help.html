TRIPLE PLATINUM

Let the good times sparkle with Triple Platinum! Win prizes by revealing Platinum Bar symbols. Reveal a Double Bar Symbol to double the prize and a Triple Bar symbol to triple the prize! When 3 or more Platinum Ring symbols are revealed, the bonus game is unlocked, giving you a chance to spin the wheel and win even more glittering prizes!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Press each Gem symbol to reveal Platinum Bar symbols, Gold Shape symbols, or Platinum Ring symbols. Reveal any Platinum Bar symbols and win the corresponding prize. Find a double Platinum Bar symbol and double the corresponding prize, and a triple Platinum Bar symbol to triple the corresponding prize.

Reveal 3 or more Platinum Ring symbols to unlock the bonus game. Press the circular arrow to spin the wheel for a chance to win more prizes. Win a prize when the pointer lands on a prize value. Prizes accumulate until the bonus ends. Advance to the next wheel with richer prizes when the pointer lands on an Arrow symbol. Landing on an X symbol removes one of the Platinum Rings accumulated in the base game. The bonus game ends when no more Platinum Rings remain.

The game ends when all symbols have been revealed and any awarded bonus is complete.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Dismiss the win message and view the game's outcome by pressing the message.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made and any prizes have been awarded.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
